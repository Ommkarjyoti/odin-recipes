Chicken Spaghetti

This chicken spaghetti recipe is quick and easy to make.

This dish has a wonderful Southern snap and yet is smooth and hearty!

Ingredients

  • One (twelve ounce) package angel hair pasta
  • Two cups chopped cooked chicken breast
  • One (ten and three fourth ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • One (ten ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  • Three fourth (eight ounce) package processed cheese, cubed
  • Half (four ounce) jar sliced mushrooms, drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook angel hair pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes.
  3. Add chopped chicken, condensed soup, diced tomatoes with chile peppers, processed cheese, mushrooms, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and mixture is heated through, about 5 minutes.
